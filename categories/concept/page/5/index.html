The locator map is a metaphor for understanding how \(X\) fits within a broader context. It’s a concept from Tanya Reilly | The Staff Engineer’s Path. \(X\) here includes, but isn’t limited to: How do you fit within your team? What is your role? How does your team fit within your company? How does it relate to other internal teams and to your company’s product/service offerings?
