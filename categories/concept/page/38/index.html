Topological order

In computer science, a topological sort or topological ordering of a directed graph [Directed graph] is a linear ordering of its vertices such that for every directed edge \(uv\) from vertex [Vertex] \(u\) to vertex \(v\), \(u\) comes before \(v\) in the ordering.
