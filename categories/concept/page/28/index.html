Commoditize Your Compliments

Commoditizing your compliments is a business strategy to ensure you maintain a dominant position and capture the majority of the surplus value involved in nearby transactions. Every product in the marketplace has substitutes and complements. A substitute is another product you might buy if the first product is too expensive. Chicken is a substitute for beef. Reasoning from first principles

First principles thinking consists of deriving things to their fundamental proven axioms [First principles] in the given arena, before reasoning up by asking which ones are relevant to the question at hand, then cross referencing conclusions based on chosen axioms and making sure conclusions don’t violate any fundamental laws.
