Big Theta notation, written as \\(\bigtheta{g(n)}\\), indicates \\(f\\) is bound above and below, asymptotically, by \\(g\\) (a tight bound).

\\[\bigtheta{n} \implies \bigo{n} \\;\text{and}\\; \bigomega{n}\\]
