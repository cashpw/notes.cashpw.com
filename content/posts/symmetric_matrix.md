> In linear algebra, a symmetric matrix is a [Square matrix]({{< relref "square_matrix.md" >}}) that is equal to its [Transpose]({{< relref "transpose.md" >}}). Formally,
>
> \\(A \text{ is symmetric} \iff A=A^{\textsf{T}}\\)
>
> (“Symmetric Matrix” 2022)
