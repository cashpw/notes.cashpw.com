> \\(\tilde{a} \overset{\Delta}{=} \vec{a} - \operatorname{avg}(\vec{a})\vec{1} = \mathbf{C}\vec{a}\\)
>
> (Boyd and Vandenberghe 2018, 60)

Note that \\(\mathbf{C}\\) is the [Centering matrix]({{< relref "centering_matrix.md" >}}).
