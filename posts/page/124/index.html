Prisoner's dilemma

The prisoner’s dilemma is a standard example of a game analyzed in Game theory that shows why two completely rational individuals might not cooperate, even if it appears that it is in their best interests to do so. (“Prisoner’s Dilemma” 2022) Two members of a criminal organization are arrested and imprisoned. Each prisoner is in solitary confinement with no means of communicating with the other.
