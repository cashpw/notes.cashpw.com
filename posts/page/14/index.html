Deep-dive reading

Deep-dive reading is my term for reading more than one source either on a particular topic or by a particular author with the goal of gaining deeper understanding. This is in contrast to Shallow-dive reading. Data-frame model

The way we understand the world (Mental models) affects which information we perceive, and vice versa (e.g. Everything looks like a nail when all you have is a hammer). This bi-directional flow is the data-frame model. Data are the interpreted signals of events; frames are the explanatory structures that account for the data.
