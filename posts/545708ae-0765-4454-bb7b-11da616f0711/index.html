This is my current One-bag travel packing list. The guiding concepts are:

  1. Think in Permutations

    Two tops and two bottoms makes four looks!

    Example: Travel clothing permutations

  2. Prefer solids to liquids

    The USA’s Transportation Security Administration (TSA) requires that “[e]ach passenger may carry liquids, gels and aerosols in travel-size containers that are 3.4 ounces or 100 milliliters. Each passenger is limited to one quart-size bag of liquids, gels and aerosols” (NO_ITEM_DATA:transportationsecurityadministrationWhat311LiquidsRule).

    Example: Bar soap rather than liquid soap

  3. Multi-purpose

    Example: A gaiter (a) serves as a scarf, face covering, headband, balaclava, sleep mask, and more!

Packing list¶

Clothing¶

I pack with the expectation that I’ll do laundry every 5 days and will wear things more than once before washing them (except underwear). This allows me to pack less clothing and overall keep weight down.

  • Tops
    • 2x wool button-down shirts
    • 2x t-shirt
    • 1x jacket/windbreaker
  • Bottoms
    • 2x pants (a)
    • 1x shorts (exercise and swim)
    • 3x socks
    • 5x boxer briefs
  • Footwear
    • 1x boots (a)
    • 3x socks (a)
    • 1x sandals (a)
  • Misc
    • 1x Lightweight merino wool Buff (a) (doubles as sleep mask)
    • 1x Sun hat (a) (ideally foldable/collapsible)
    • 1x Belt (a)
    • 1x sunglasses

Storage¶

  • 1x backpack (a) (ideally water-proof/resistant)
  • 1x sling bag (ideally theft-resistant)
  • ?x zip-able packing bags
  • 3x gallon zip-lock bags
  • 3x quart zip-lock bags

Accessories¶

  • 1x umbrella
  • 1x water bottle (collapsible)
  • 1x face mask
  • 1x pair concert earplugs
  • 4x pairs sleeping earplugs
  • 1x travel utensil spork/etc

Documentation¶

  • 1x passport
  • 1x USA Drivers license

Technology¶

  • 1x laptop Essential for how I read (example), study, and write these notes
  • 1x USB adapter/converter (a) Can charge everything I bring with at once and I can plug it in everywhere1 I go
  • 2x external USB batteries (a)
  • 1x phone (Pixel 6 Pro)
  • 1x watch (Google Pixel Watch (a))
  • Cables
    • 2x USB-C to USB-C cables (a)
    • USB-A to USB-micro-B cable
    • Watch charging cable

  • 1x debit card

Food¶

There’s no way to one-bag if I bring these. I bring an additional duffel bag to hold the food.

  • ?x protein powder (a) and bars (a) to total 100-200g protein per day It’s difficult to source sufficient macro-aligned vegan protein while traveling.
  • 1x protein shaker

Exercise¶

  • Lacrosse ball (a): Roll out my tight muscles
  • Resistance band: Bodyweight Exercise program
  • Yoga band (a): Stretch and general rope/tying utility
