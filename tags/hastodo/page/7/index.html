It can often be described compactly, as a function that maps (in constant time) the properties of an edge/turn into a cost. Topology (Math)

In mathematics, topology is concerned with the properties of a geometric [Geometry] object that are preserved under continuous deformations, such as stretching, twisting, crumpling, and bending; that is, without closing holes, opening holes, tearing, gluing, or passing through itself.
