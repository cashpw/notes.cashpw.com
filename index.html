This website contains my personal notes and thoughts. The key component of my notes are the links which connect them. Links allow forward movement (e.g. A to B). Backlinks allow backward movement (e.g. B to A). These sorts of interconnected notes are sometimes called zettelkasten, a second brain, a digital garden. They are a type of personal knowledge management system.

I write these notes for myself and share them online as a way of increasing accountability for myself. The public nature of the notes encourages me to think carefully about my own thoughts and how I write.
